# Genre Profile: Casual

Casual games are the native genre of the source template. All 15 chapters apply.
Emphasis: short sessions, single-hand operation, ad monetization as primary revenue,
low learning cost, strong immediate feedback, and accessibility for non-gamers.

## Questioning Emphasis

- Core loop questions should focus on session length (30-90 sec) and replay motivation
- Monetization questions should lead with ad strategy -- rewarded video placement, interstitial frequency, banner policy
- Difficulty should emphasize accessibility over challenge -- failure must feel gentle, not punishing
- Onboarding should prioritize immediate positive feedback within 10 seconds of first touch
- Content questions should address volume production -- how to sustain 100+ levels without exhausting design space
