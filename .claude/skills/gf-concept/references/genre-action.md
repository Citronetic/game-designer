# Genre Profile: Action

Action games emphasize real-time controls, physical feedback, combat feel, reflexes,
and precise input. Sessions vary (5-30 min). Revenue model depends on platform --
mobile may use ads, PC/console uses premium or IAP. Control design, combat feedback,
and performance requirements are the critical pillars.

## Questioning Emphasis

- Chapter 3: Controls are everything -- what does movement feel like? How does combat connect? What makes hits feel impactful? What is the combo/ability system?
- Chapter 5: Enemy variety and level/arena design -- what forces the player to use different tactics? How do set-piece encounters work?
- Chapter 6: Difficulty tuning is make-or-break -- how do difficulty modes work? How is enemy damage/health tuned? Are there i-frames or dodge mechanics?
- Chapter 7: Teaching controls through doing, not reading -- how does the first encounter teach attack, dodge, and special abilities?
- Chapter 11: Visual feedback is part of the gameplay -- how do hit effects, screen shake, slow-motion, and particles communicate impact?
- Chapter 13: Performance is gameplay -- what are frame rate targets? What is acceptable input latency? How do physics and hitboxes work?
