# Genre Profile: Idle

Idle games center on offline progression, prestige/reset mechanics, and exponential
growth systems. Active sessions are brief (1-5 min check-ins) with the core appeal
being "progress while away." Revenue is hybrid -- ads for acceleration and IAP for
premium boosts. Economy loop design and prestige systems are the critical pillars.

## Questioning Emphasis

- Chapter 3: What does the player actually do during the brief active session? What makes tapping/upgrading feel rewarding?
- Chapter 4: The idle loop IS the game -- how does offline accumulation work? When and why does the player prestige? What resets and what persists?
- Chapter 6: Exponential scaling and wall placement define the entire experience -- how fast does progress slow? When do walls appear? How aggressive is the prestige incentive?
- Chapter 8: Notification-driven re-engagement -- what pulls the player back? How does offline reward collection feel satisfying?
- Chapter 9: If using ads -- how do rewarded videos integrate with the idle loop (2x speed, offline bonus multiplier)?
- Chapter 10: Permanent multipliers and convenience purchases -- how does spending create lasting value without breaking the scaling?
