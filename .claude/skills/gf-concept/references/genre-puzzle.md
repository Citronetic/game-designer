# Genre Profile: Puzzle

Puzzle games focus on cognitive challenge, pattern recognition, and satisfying "aha"
moments. Sessions are moderate (2-10 min per puzzle). Revenue is typically hybrid --
ads for free users, IAP for remove-ads and hint packs. Level design and difficulty
curves are the most critical design areas.

## Questioning Emphasis

- Chapter 3: What is the core puzzle mechanic? What makes it feel novel vs existing puzzles? How large is the solution space per puzzle?
- Chapter 5: Level design is the product -- how are puzzles authored? Can any be procedurally generated? What prevents content exhaustion?
- Chapter 6: Difficulty curve is the single most important design axis -- how do you teach new mechanics while maintaining challenge? What is the hint philosophy?
- Chapter 7: Puzzle games must teach the mechanic through play, not text -- how does the first puzzle teach the rules?
- Chapter 10: Hint packs and remove-ads are the primary IAP -- how does the free experience remain fair without purchases?
